An adroit approach for Text Summarisation

Abstract

Text summarisation condenses a text to a shorter version while retaining the important information. This is important as it saves a lot of time compared to reading the whole document, text summarisation is also an integral part of many technologies. In this endeavour of ours, we put forth a new extractive method for text summarization which combines the important aspects involved in key phrase extraction and semantic distribution of sentences. The paper also presents that the proposed method achieves a performance at par or better with present recognized models and methods. The model is proposed for its use in real-time applications. We report our results in document modeling using the proposed model. The novelty in the approach is the distribution of semantic meaning throughout the summary in order to include the whole document.
