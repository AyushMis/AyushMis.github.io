Study and Analysis of Wighted Based Pagerank Method

Abstract

In this era of endless information and technology, people use the available sources like Google to find out information which uses the PageRank Algorithm as the basis for its search engine. Now the race is to find the more efficient and speedy algorithm to get our results as fast as possible. In this research paper, we aim to introduce a new algorithm to find out the page ranks. We have introduced the concept of weighted matrices which considers the weight of all the in and out links to a webpage. The results obtained were compared with the existing algorithms of PageRank and Eigen Vector centrality using the Kendall Tau correlation coefficient and we found that the current method not only provided the correct results but more efficient ones.
